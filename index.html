About Me

Hi my name is Mads Olsen

I am a young man living in Svendborg, currently taking a multimedia design education at UCL Odense. In my spare time I like being on the internet and play videogames. One of my strong suits is being creative and cooperating with out-of-the-box thinking in my problem solving. My goal is to one day become a web designer with a focus on coding, because it's what I like working with the most.
